South Africa

  • Motto Unity in Diversity
  • Nickname Rainbow Nation

Fun facts :

  • Which one is it ? South Africa has three capital cities – Cape Town (Legislative), Pretoria (Administrative) and Bloemfontein (Judicial)
  • Dark Past, Bright Future For nearly 50 years, there was a state of apartheid (white minority rule) in South Africa, it has been abolished in 1993
  • Yukon in Africa South Africa was once the world’s number one producer of gold
  • Versatile South Africa has: deserts, wetlands, grasslands, bush, subtropical forests, mountains and escarpments
