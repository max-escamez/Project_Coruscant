Ireland

  • Motto One Hundred Thousand Welcomes
  • Nickname The Emerald Isle

Fun facts :

  • Lineage The very common prefix Mc/Mac means "son of" and " O' " means "descendant of"
  • The Guinness Culture The Irish consume in average 131.1 liters of beer per year
  • Thanks For Nothing St. Patrick’s clearing of the Emerald Isle of snakes isn’t true. There have never been any snakes in Ireland
  • Far From Home It is estimated that over 80 million people of Irish descent live outside Ireland. This is 14 times more than the population of Ireland
