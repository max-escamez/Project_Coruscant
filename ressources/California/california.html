Fun facts :

  • Crowded State One out of every eight United States residents lives in California
  • Movieland The first motion picture theater opened in Los Angeles on April 2, 1902
  • The King of all trees Sequoia National Park contains the largest living tree. It's named Hyperion and its trunk is 102 feet in circumference
  • Still on the flag California was originally known as the Grizzly Bear State. As the bear population was wiped out it became the Golden State
